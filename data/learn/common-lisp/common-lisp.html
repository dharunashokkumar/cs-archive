Awesome Common Lisp

A curated list of awesome Common Lisp libraries.

For awesome software, see lisp-lang.org's success stories and the awesome-cl-software list.

All libraries listed here are available from [Quicklisp][16] unless stated otherwise. The ones marked with a ‚≠ê are so widespread and solid that they became community standards. You can't be wrong with them. This is the case for Quicklisp, BordeauxThreads and such. Libraries denoted with a üëç are the ones we like and want to promote here at the Awesome-cl list. They proved solid, they may solve a problem better than a community standard but they aren't as widespread, or not considered as stable. For example, we prefer Spinneret over Cl-Who.

Add something new! See the contributing section for adding something to the list.
