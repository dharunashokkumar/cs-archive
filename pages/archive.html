Content Index

  • Structure and Interpretation of Computer Programs (SICP) (Book / Course) - The Wizard Book. A fundamental textbook on computer programming from MIT.
  • The Computer Chronicles (1983-2002) (Video Series) - A golden window into the history of personal computing. See the launch of the Mac, Windows 1.0, and the Internet.
  • UNIX Programmer's Manual (1979) (Documentation) - The original v7 UNIX manual. A masterclass in technical writing and system design.
  • Hackers: Heroes of the Computer Revolution (Audiobook/Text) - Steven Levy's classic book about the early culture of hackers at MIT and beyond.
  • SimCity (1989) (Software) - Play the original SimCity directly in your browser via DOSBox emulation.
  • Xerox Alto Operating System (Software) - Experience the GUI that inspired Macintosh and Windows.
